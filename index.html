Piece of cake !

You know the feeling when you screw up and need to start all over to figure something out? (yes like every command) Well, no more! With one simple command you can put Arch back with a clean slate on your sd card. Enjoy and donate if you like what I'm doing!

RPI 2 Arch.

Insert a RPI sd card and copy the command below into your linux terminal.
Let 'er rip !

bash <(curl -s https://ackoujens.github.io/archlinux-raspberry-pi-2-install/script.sh)
